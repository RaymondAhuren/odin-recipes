Grilled Teriyaki Shrimp and Pineapple Skewers




Grilled Teriyaki Shrimp and Pineapple Skewers offer a delightful combination of flavors that make for a delicious
and visually appealing dish. Succulent shrimp, marinated in a tangy teriyaki sauce, are paired with juicy pineapple
chunks and grilled to perfection on skewers.

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup water
  • ⅓ 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar/li>
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh garlic
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 pound jumbo shrimp
  • ½ fresh pineapple, cored and cut into 1 1/2-inch pieces
  • skewers
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh cilantro (Optional)
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds (Optional)

Steps

Step 1

Combine water, soy sauce, brown sugar, honey,
garlic, and ginger in a small saucepan and bring to a boil over
medium-high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer
until sauce has reduced and thickened slightly, 8 to 10 minutes.

Step 2

Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil grate.
Thread shrimp and pineapple alternately onto skewers and place on a platter.

Step 3

Arrange skewers on the hot grate. Grill 2 to 3 minutes per side, or until shrimp
is opaque and cooked through. Turn grill to low heat and brush sauce on both
sides of skewers. Transfer to a serving platter and sprinkle with cilantro and sesame seeds.
